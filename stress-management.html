Stress Management Exercise

This exercise assesses your ability to recognize, manage, and cope with stress effectively. Effective stress management involves identifying stressors, understanding your stress responses, and using healthy coping strategies. Answer based on your typical reactions and behaviors.

1. How do you typically recognize when you're experiencing stress?

2. When you feel stressed, what is your immediate response?

3. How do you prioritize tasks when you're feeling overwhelmed?

4. What coping strategies do you use when stressed?

5. How well can you identify what's causing your stress?

6. How do you handle unexpected stressful situations?

7. How do you maintain work-life balance during stressful periods?

8. How do you feel about asking for help when you're stressed?

9. How does stress affect your sleep?

10. How quickly do you recover from stressful situations?
