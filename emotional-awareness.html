Emotional Awareness Exercise

This exercise will help you assess your ability to recognize, understand, and reflect on your own emotions. Answer each question honestly based on how you typically respond in various situations. There are no right or wrong answersâ€”this is about self-awareness.

1. When you experience a strong emotion, how quickly do you recognize what you're feeling?

2. How do you typically respond when someone asks "How are you feeling?"

3. When you feel upset, what do you usually do first?

4. How often do you reflect on your emotional patterns and triggers?

5. Can you distinguish between similar emotions (e.g., anxiety vs. excitement)?

6. How aware are you of physical sensations that accompany your emotions?

7. When you experience conflicting emotions, how do you handle it?

8. How well can you identify the root cause of your emotions?

9. How do you feel about expressing your emotions to others?

10. How often do you check in with yourself about your emotional state?
