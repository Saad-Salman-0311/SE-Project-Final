Empathy Practice Exercise

This exercise evaluates your ability to understand, share, and respond to the emotions of others. Empathy involves recognizing emotional cues, perspective-taking, and responding with compassion. Answer based on your typical behavior in social situations.

1. When someone is visibly upset, what is your first reaction?

2. How well can you read non-verbal cues (facial expressions, body language)?

3. When someone shares a problem with you, how do you typically respond?

4. How often do you try to see situations from other people's perspectives?

5. When someone's emotions differ from yours in a situation, how do you react?

6. How do you respond when someone is celebrating a success?

7. How well can you sense when someone needs emotional support?

8. When someone is angry, how do you typically respond?

9. How do you feel when you see someone else in pain or distress?

10. How often do you consider how your words or actions might affect others emotionally?
