About Emotional Intelligence

What is EI?

Emotional Intelligence (EI) is the ability to recognize, understand, and manage our own emotions, as well as recognize, understand, and influence the emotions of others. It's a crucial skill for personal and professional success.

Why It Matters

When combined with effective goal-setting strategies, EI becomes a powerful tool. This project explores how emotional awareness, self-regulation, empathy, and social skills can enhance your ability to set meaningful goals and achieve lasting success.
